The Friends of Brooklyn Park Summer Youth Program purpose is to engage youth in healthy games, activities, and crafts; to provide guidance and a safe place for children to learn and explore their interests; to teach them the value of community.

Our mission is to provide the Brooklyn neighborhood and Portland community opportunities to engage, learn, and explore Brooklyn Park and fund and operate a summer program serving youth.

Our mission is also to create a welcoming and safe park for Brooklyn and the greater community and build a working relationship with Portland Parks and Recreation and the Portland Parks Foundation.
